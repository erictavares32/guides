10 Gripping TV Shows Like The Last of Us

If you loved The Last of Us for its emotional storytelling, post-apocalyptic setting, and complex character relationships, you'll enjoy these similarly compelling series. From zombie outbreaks to pandemic survival stories, these shows explore humanity at its best and worst in extraordinary circumstances.

Chernobyl

Historical Drama

Available on HBO Max, Apple TV

A haunting dramatization of the 1986 nuclear disaster, Chernobyl explores the chilling consequences of bureaucratic negligence and heroism amidst chaos. Like The Last of Us, it showcases ordinary people facing extraordinary catastrophes, with visceral storytelling that captures psychological horror and the tragic fallout of disaster.

The Leftovers

Psychological Drama

Available on HBO Max, Hulu

When 2% of the world's population vanishes without explanation, survivors struggle to cope. The Leftovers is a profound exploration of grief, faith, and human connection in a fractured reality. Fans of The Last of Us' emotional depth will appreciate its surreal yet moving examination of how people endure unimaginable loss.

The Walking Dead

Zombie Apocalypse

Available on Netflix, AMC+

The iconic zombie series that defined survival horror television. The Walking Dead explores ever-evolving threats (both undead and human) with gripping stories about loss, leadership, and maintaining humanity in a lawless world. Its early seasons particularly mirror The Last of Us' themes of found family and moral dilemmas in crisis.

Station Eleven

Post-Apocalyptic Drama

Available on HBO Max

Following a devastating flu pandemic, Station Eleven follows a troupe of Shakespearean actors navigating a fractured world. This haunting, poetic series explores hope, art, and memory - much like The Last of Us' emphasis on culture's survival. Its nonlinear storytelling reveals how trauma connects people across generations.

Black Summer

Survival Horror

Available on Netflix

This pulse-pounding zombie thriller throws viewers directly into societal collapse. Black Summer captures the visceral fear and desperation of survival with raw intensity. Its grounded approach to the zombie genre and focus on human behavior under extreme stress will appeal to The Last of Us fans.

Sweet Tooth

Fantasy Adventure

Available on Netflix

A whimsical yet emotional tale of a post-virus world where hybrid children emerge. Sweet Tooth blends wonder with deep human drama as a boy-deer hybrid and his protector journey together. The evolving father-son dynamic directly parallels Joel and Ellie's relationship in The Last of Us.

Children of the Whales

Anime Drama

Available on Netflix

This visually stunning anime follows island-dwellers adrift in a sea of sand. Children of the Whales explores memory, war, and emotional growth in a world where feelings have consequences. Like The Last of Us, it examines how societies rebuild and the costs of survival through beautiful, melancholic storytelling.

Snowpiercer

Dystopian Thriller

Available on Netflix, TNT

In a frozen apocalyptic world, humanity's last survivors live aboard a perpetually moving train. Snowpiercer dissects class warfare and revolution in a claustrophobic dystopia. Its examination of social hierarchies under extreme conditions resonates with The Last of Us' themes of power and morality.

12 Monkeys

Sci-Fi Thriller

Available on Hulu

This time travel thriller follows a man sent back to prevent a deadly pandemic. 12 Monkeys evolves from a virus story into a complex tale of fate and sacrifice. Its emotional character arcs and high-stakes survival scenarios will satisfy The Last of Us fans who enjoy intricate storytelling.

The 100

Post-Apocalyptic Adventure

Available on Netflix

Juvenile delinquents return to Earth a century after nuclear apocalypse. The 100 explores moral dilemmas and society-building in a dangerous new world. Its themes of leadership, survival, and the costs of violence make it compelling for fans of The Last of Us' darker moments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What shows are most similar to The Last of Us?

The most similar shows to The Last of Us include The Walking Dead for its survival themes and zombie threats, Station Eleven for its emotional post-pandemic storytelling and focus on art's survival, and The Leftovers for its profound exploration of grief and human connection after catastrophe.

Where can I watch these shows like The Last of Us?

All shows listed include direct streaming availability links. Most are available on major platforms: HBO Max (Chernobyl, The Leftovers, Station Eleven), Netflix (Black Summer, Sweet Tooth, The 100), and Hulu (12 Monkeys). Availability may vary by region - use our links for precise streaming information in your country.

Are there any anime similar to The Last of Us?

Yes, Children of the Whales offers a unique anime perspective with similar themes of survival in a harsh world and the importance of human connections. For more action-oriented anime with post-apocalyptic settings, consider Attack on Titan or Dr. Stone, though they differ tonally from The Last of Us.

Which show has the best father-daughter relationship like Joel and Ellie?

Sweet Tooth features a touching surrogate father-son relationship between Gus and Tommy that evolves similarly to Joel and Ellie's bond. The Mandalorian (Disney+) also explores similar guardian-child dynamics, though in a sci-fi setting rather than post-apocalyptic.

Are these shows appropriate for teenagers?

Most shows here are rated TV-MA for violence and mature themes like The Last of Us. Sweet Tooth is the most family-friendly option (TV-14), while others like Chernobyl and The Walking Dead contain intense, graphic scenes. We recommend checking Common Sense Media or similar resources for detailed age-appropriateness guidance.
