10 Must-Watch Series If You Loved The Last of Us

Chernobyl

A haunting dramatization of the 1986 nuclear disaster, Chernobyl explores the chilling consequences of bureaucratic negligence and heroism amidst chaos. With visceral storytelling, it captures the psychological horror and tragic fallout of one of history's worst man-made catastrophes.

The Leftovers

When 2% of the world's population vanishes without explanation, the world struggles to cope. The Leftovers is a moving, surreal exploration of grief, faith, and human connection in a fractured reality where nothing can ever be the same again.

The Walking Dead

The Walking Dead is the iconic zombie apocalypse series that redefined survival horror. With ever-evolving threats, both undead and human, it tells gripping stories of loss, leadership, and what it means to stay human in a lawless world.

Station Eleven

Following a devastating flu pandemic, Station Eleven follows a troupe of Shakespearean actors navigating a fractured world. Haunting and poetic, it explores hope, art, memory, and the power of storytelling to heal and connect across generations.

Black Summer

A pulse-pounding zombie thriller, Black Summer throws viewers into the chaos of a society unraveling. With raw intensity and minimal exposition, it captures the visceral fear and desperation of survival at the brink of societal collapse.

Sweet Tooth

Sweet Tooth is a whimsical yet emotional tale of a post-virus world where hybrid children are born. It blends adventure, wonder, and deep human emotion as a boy-deer hybrid and a rugged survivor journey to uncover his origins.

Children of the Whales

Set on a mystical floating island adrift in sand, Children of the Whales is a visually striking anime exploring memory, war, and emotional growth. It dives deep into society, fate, and rebellion in a world where emotions come at a cost.

Snowpiercer

In a frozen Earth, Snowpiercer rides the last survivors aboard a perpetually moving train. The show dissects class warfare, revolution, and survival inside a claustrophobic, dystopian society ruled by secrets and systems of control.

12 Monkeys

A gripping time travel thriller, 12 Monkeys follows a man sent back to prevent a deadly pandemic. What begins as a mission to stop a virus evolves into a tangled tale of paradoxes, destiny, and the cost of changing time.

The 100

The 100 tells the story of juvenile survivors sent to Earth a century after a nuclear apocalypse. Facing harsh realities and moral dilemmas, they must build new societies and make impossible choices in a world as dangerous as it is beautiful.
