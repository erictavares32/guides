Top 10 Amazon Prime Video Shows You Must Watch (May 2025)

Amazon Prime Video continues to deliver exceptional original content in 2025. From post-apocalyptic dramas to fantasy epics and witty comedies, here are the 10 best Amazon Prime shows you shouldn't miss this month.

Fallout

Post-Apocalyptic Drama

Based on the popular video game series, Fallout presents a gripping narrative set in a post-nuclear war America, exploring themes of survival and humanity. The show brings the game's iconic retro-futuristic aesthetic to life while introducing new characters and stories.

The Boys: Gen V

Superhero, Drama, Comedy

A spinoff from The Boys, this series follows young superheroes at Godolkin University as they navigate their powers and moral complexities. The show maintains the franchise's signature dark humor while exploring the challenges of young adulthood with superpowers.

The Wheel of Time

Fantasy

An adaptation of Robert Jordan's epic fantasy novels, The Wheel of Time follows Moiraine as she embarks on a journey to find the Dragon Reborn, who is prophesied to save or destroy the world. The series expands its rich mythology in its third season.

Reacher

Action, Thriller

Based on Lee Child's novels, Reacher stars Alan Ritchson as Jack Reacher, a former military policeman who uncovers dangerous conspiracies while traveling across the U.S. The new season adapts another thrilling book from the series.

The Legend of Vox Machina

Animated Fantasy

An animated series featuring a group of misfit adventurers on a quest to save the realm of Exandria from dark magical forces. The Legend of Vox Machina continues to delight fans with its perfect blend of fantasy storytelling and humor.

Fleabag

Comedy, Drama

A witty and emotionally raw series created by and starring Phoebe Waller-Bridge, portraying a woman navigating life and love in London. Fleabag remains one of Prime Video's most acclaimed shows years after its finale.

The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel

Comedy, Drama

Set in the 1950s, the series follows Miriam "Midge" Maisel, a housewife who pursues a career in stand-up comedy after her marriage falls apart. The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel concluded its five-season run with critical acclaim.

Étoile

Drama

A ballet drama from Amy Sherman-Palladino, exploring the lives of dancers and the challenges they face in the world of professional ballet. Étoile brings the same sharp dialogue and character depth as The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel.

Mr. & Mrs. Smith

Action, Comedy

A reimagining of the 2005 film, this series stars Donald Glover and Maya Erskine as a married couple who are also spies, balancing domestic life with high-stakes missions. The show offers a fresh take on the spy genre with humor and heart.

The Second Best Hospital in the Galaxy

Animated Sci-Fi Comedy

An animated series that satirizes the medical drama genre, set in a futuristic hospital dealing with bizarre and humorous intergalactic medical cases. The Second Best Hospital in the Galaxy features an all-star voice cast and clever writing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best Amazon Prime Video shows to watch in 2025?

Our top picks for May 2025 include Fallout, The Boys: Gen V, The Wheel of Time, Reacher, and The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel. These shows represent the best of Amazon's original programming across various genres.

Is Fallout based on the video game?

Yes, Fallout is based on the popular video game series and presents a gripping narrative set in post-nuclear war America. The show expands on the game's lore while staying true to its retro-futuristic aesthetic and dark humor.

Is The Boys: Gen V connected to The Boys?

Yes, The Boys: Gen V is a spinoff from The Boys, following young superheroes at Godolkin University. While it stands on its own, it shares the same universe and occasionally crosses over with characters from the main series.

How many seasons of The Wheel of Time are available?

As of May 2025, The Wheel of Time has three seasons available on Amazon Prime Video, with each season adapting one of Robert Jordan's epic fantasy novels.

Is Fleabag available on Amazon Prime?

Yes, Fleabag is available on Amazon Prime Video and is highly acclaimed for its witty and emotional storytelling. Both seasons of this award-winning series are available to stream.
