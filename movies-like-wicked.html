10 Magical Movies Like Wicked (2024)

If you were captivated by Wicked's magical world, powerful female leads, and spectacular musical numbers, you'll love these 10 similar movies. From reimagined fairy tales to fantastical musicals, these films capture the spirit of Wicked's untold stories, complex friendships, and exploration of good versus evil.

Wicked (2024) - Quick Overview

Director: Jon M. Chu
Stars: Cynthia Erivo, Ariana Grande, Jeff Goldblum
Release: November 22, 2024
Genre: Musical Fantasy

This two-part cinematic adaptation of the Broadway musical reimagines the Land of Oz through the lens of Elphaba (the Wicked Witch of the West) and Glinda the Good Witch's complex friendship. The story explores themes of identity, prejudice, and how society labels "good" and "evil," set against spectacular musical numbers and stunning visuals.
